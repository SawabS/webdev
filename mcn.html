Raspberry Pi 4: A Compact Computer




Overview

Meet the Raspberry Pi 4, a compact powerhouse built for versatility. With its faster processor, expanded memory, and broad connectivity options, it's perfect for projects ranging from coding and robotics to media centers and home automation. Easy to set up and packed with advanced features, it’s your go-to tool for turning ideas into reality.

Key Features

  • Blazing Fast Performance: Powered by a Quad-core Cortex-A72 (ARM v8) 64-bit SoC, the Raspberry Pi 4 delivers speeds up to 1.5GHz, making it 3x faster than its predecessor.
  • Enhanced Memory Options: Choose from 2GB, 4GB, or 8GB of LPDDR4-3200 SDRAM to meet your specific needs, whether you're working on simple projects or handling more intensive tasks.
  • Dual 4K Display Support: Two micro-HDMI ports support dual displays at resolutions up to 4K, ideal for multimedia applications, gaming, or professional workspaces.
  • Fast Networking: Built-in Gigabit Ethernet and dual-band 802.11ac wireless networking offer faster and more stable internet connectivity.
  • USB 3.0 Ports: Transfer data at lightning speeds with two USB 3.0 ports, alongside two USB 2.0 ports for connecting peripherals like keyboards and mice.
  • Bluetooth 5.0: Seamlessly connect wireless devices, such as keyboards, mice, and headphones, with enhanced speed and range.

Make Your Projects Come To Life

The Raspberry Pi 4 isn’t just a computer; it’s a gateway to limitless possibilities. Whether you're a hobbyist, a student, or a professional developer, this little board has the power and flexibility to support your projects, including:

  • Coding & Learning: An excellent tool for learning programming languages like Python, C++, and JavaScript.
  • DIY Electronics Projects: Perfect for creating gadgets, home automation systems, or IoT devices.
  • Media Centers: Build your own home theater with support for 4K video playback and powerful audio capabilities.
  • Retro Gaming Consoles: Relive classic gaming experiences with emulator software.
  • Robotics: Ideal for powering your robotics projects with easy integration and extensive community support.

Easy to Set Up, Ready to Go

Setting up the Raspberry Pi 4 is straightforward, even for beginners. Just connect it to a monitor, keyboard, and mouse, install an operating system like Raspberry Pi OS, and you’re ready to explore its capabilities. The extensive online resources and community support make troubleshooting and learning a breeze.

Technical Specifications

  • Processor: Quad-core Cortex-A72 (ARM v8) 64-bit SoC @ 1.5GHz
  • Memory: 2GB, 4GB, or 8GB LPDDR4-3200 SDRAM
  • Connectivity:
    • 2 × USB 3.0 ports
    • 2 × USB 2.0 ports
    • Gigabit Ethernet
    • 802.11ac wireless networking
    • Bluetooth 5.0
  • Video Output: 2 × micro-HDMI ports (up to 4Kp60 supported)
  • Audio: 4-pole stereo audio and composite video port, HDMI, and Bluetooth audio support
  • Storage: MicroSD card slot for operating system and data storage
  • Power: USB-C power supply (5V/3A recommended)
